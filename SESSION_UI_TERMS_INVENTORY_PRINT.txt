5) Proposed 3-Button Category Model (Next Iteration)
Button WHERE
- Primary question: Where am I in Session?
- Term focus: track_index, track_name, slot_index, selected_track, selected_scene, highlighted_clip_slot
- Template: Track {index}: {name}. Slot {index}.

Button WHAT
- Primary question: What is in this slot?
- Term focus: has_clip, clip_name, clip_id, clip_length, looping, loop_start, loop_end
- Template: Clip: {name or Empty}. Length: {length}. Loop: {on/off}, {start} to {end}.

Button STATE
- Primary question: What is it doing right now?
- Term focus: status, is_recording, is_playing, Recording, Playing, Stopped
- Template: Status: {Stopped|Playing|Recording}.
